Privacy at Nexus Group AI

At Nexus Group AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

We believe in privacy by design and only collect data that helps us improve our services and provide you with the best possible experience.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Contact Forms: Name, email address, company, and message content
  • Newsletter Signup: Email address and preferences
  • Demo Requests: Name, email, company details, and specific requirements

Analytics Information (with your consent):

  • Website Usage: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address (anonymized)
  • Performance Data: Page load times, user interactions

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This service:

  • Anonymizes your IP address
  • Does not share data with third parties for advertising
  • Requires your explicit consent before activation
  • Can be disabled at any time using our analytics settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Personal Information:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you newsletters and updates (only with your consent)
  • Provide demos and technical information about IRIS
  • Improve our products and services based on feedback

Analytics Information:

  • Understand website usage patterns and popular content
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Identify technical issues and optimize loading times
  • Make data-driven decisions about content and features

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Consent

Newsletter subscriptions, analytics tracking, and marketing communications

Contract Performance

Providing demos, technical support, and fulfilling service requests

Legitimate Interest

Website security, fraud prevention, and business operations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (with anonymized IP)
  • Email Services: Newsletter delivery and communication
  • Hosting Provider: Website hosting and security

Legal Requirements:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
