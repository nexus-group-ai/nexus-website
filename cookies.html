Cookies at Nexus Group AI

This Cookie Policy ("Policy") explains how Nexus Group AI and its subsidiaries (collectively referred to as "Nexus," "we," "us," and "our") use cookies and similar technologies when you visit our websites ("Sites") or interact with us, such as through email communications.

This Policy is part of our Privacy Policy and complies with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites recognize returning visitors, remember preferences, and enhance the overall user experience.

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They enable basic website functionality during your visit.

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device until you delete them or they expire. They remember your preferences across multiple visits.

2. Who Controls Cookies?

First-Party Cookies

Set and controlled by Nexus Group AI directly. These cookies are essential for our website's functionality and your user experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Set and controlled by third parties, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

Purpose: These cookies are essential for the website's functionality (e.g., navigation, secure access, filling forms, maintaining user sessions).

Can I disable them? No, disabling these cookies may impact the functionality of the website.
