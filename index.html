What is IRIS?

The Intelligence Resilience Information System (IRIS) harnesses advanced AI to protect critical infrastructure. By integrating Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) and cross-sectoral scenario modeling, IRIS provides the real-time risk intelligence you need to anticipate, mitigate, and recover from crises efficiently.

Core Capabilities

Immediate Cross-Sectoral Insights

Gain comprehensive risk awareness across interdependent sectors like energy, finance, and telecommunications to respond effectively to emerging threats.

Tailored Risk Analysis

Get customized threat detection and vulnerability identification aligned with your specific sector and operational needs, ensuring relevant and actionable intelligence.

Perception Analysis

Understand the nuanced context of risk reporting on a global scale to stay ahead of potential crises with data-driven recommendations.

Intuitive Visualization

Simplify complex scenarios with interactive, dynamic graphical representations, making critical data easy to understand and act upon.
