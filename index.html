What is IRIS?

The Intelligence Resilience Information System (IRIS) harnesses advanced AI to protect critical infrastructure. By integrating Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) and cross-sectoral scenario modeling, IRIS provides the real-time risk intelligence you need to anticipate, mitigate, and recover from crises efficiently.
